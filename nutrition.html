Nutrition Guide

Balanced Diet Essentials

Every meal should include a balance of:

  • Carbohydrates: Whole grains, fruits, and vegetables
  • Proteins:Lean meats, dairy, legumes
  • Fats: Healthy fats from nuts, seeds, and avocados
  • Prep snacks in advance to have healthy options available.

Meal Planning Tips

To eat healthy, consider the following tips:

  • Plan meals ahead of time to avoid unhealthy choices.
  • Incorporate a variety of foods to ensure balanced nutrition.
  • Prep snacks in advance to have healthy options available.

Hydration

Staying hydrated is essential for overall health. Aim for:

  • At least 8 cups (64 ounces) of water daily.
  • More if you're active or in a hot climate.
  • Include water-rich foods like fruits and vegetables.
